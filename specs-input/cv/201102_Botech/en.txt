
Title: Water/Power Cost Distribution System

Design, development and maintenance of a system for distributing cold water, hot water and electricity costs across apartments and shared washrooms in a housing association, based on individual meter readings. I also added encrypted communication support for integration with Bewator's access control system managing the shared washrooms.

The system integrates with water, power and access control meters and is implemented in C++/PHP on a Linux/Apache/MySQL stack, with source in Subversion deployed manually to the Linux server.

Planning followed Kanban.
